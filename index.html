About me

Ivy Tsui is an award winning graphic designer with over 20 years of experience. She specializes in promotional content and illustrations. Education: Ivy graduated from Mountain Vista High School and went on to Rhode Island School of Design. She majored in graphic design, doubling in business as well. After school, she had the opportunity to travel around Europe, visiting major art museums for inspiration. Background: She was born in St. Maarten and raised in Colorado. She enjoys hiking, running, playing tennis, and staying active in general.
